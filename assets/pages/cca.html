Course Overview

The Certificate in Computer Applications (CCA) at Tech Heaven is designed for individuals who wish to develop a solid foundation in computer applications. This course covers essential skills, office software, and basic digital literacy, making it ideal for beginners looking to enter the IT world.

Course Syllabus

3 months

Introduction to Computers, Parts of Computers, Computer Basics, MS Word, Typing Sessions

6 months

MS Excel, MS Office, MS Paint, Introduction to Internet

12 months

Multimedia and Graphics, Windows 10 Shortcuts, Ethics and Digital Citizenship

  • Junior Administrative Support
