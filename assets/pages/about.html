About Tech Heaven

Welcome to Tech Heaven, where we are committed to providing quality education in the field of computer applications. Established in the scenic city of Kotdwar, Uttarakhand, we strive to empower students with the knowledge and skills required to thrive in the tech-driven world.

We are proud to be registered with DITRP, an ISO-certified agency that ensures our curriculum and operations meet the highest standards of education. Our team of experienced educators is dedicated to delivering a top-notch syllabus tailored to the needs of aspiring IT professionals.

At Tech Heaven, our mission is simple – to make quality computer education accessible to everyone. Our students benefit from our carefully crafted courses, designed to equip them with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

With a focus on real-world applications and career growth, we offer a wide range of courses that cater to beginners, intermediates, and advanced learners. Whether you’re starting from scratch or looking to advance your IT career, Tech Heaven has something for you.

Our Location

We are proudly based in Uttarakhand, India, and serve students across the region and beyond.
